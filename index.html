Muslims in ML Community

Muslims In ML (MusIML) is an community of muslim researchers dedicated to amplifying the voices of Muslim researchers in the fields of machine learning and artificial intelligence and addressing challenges and research topics that are particularly relevant to Muslims.
We focus on both the potential for advancement and harm to Muslims and those in Muslim-majority countries who religiously identify, culturally associate, or are classified by proximity, as “Muslim”.

Our next workshop, The 6th Muslims in ML Workshop will be held in Seoul, South Korea at ICML 2026.
Also, we are planning to organize socials and mentoring sessions in NeurIPS 2026 and AISTATS 2026.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create more equitable and impactful scientific contributions by Muslim communities in machine learning to foster meaningful research and innovation.

We envision a world in which Muslims and those in Muslim-majority regions are empowered to engage in, contribute to, and benefit from global scientific discourse in machine learning.

Through high-quality research, sustained dialogue, and cross-sector collaboration, we seek to advance research and promote researchers from Muslim backgrounds to meaningfully shape the development of AI systems that serve global society.

Our Mission

Muslims in ML provides a platform for the machine learning community to advance research, foster dialogue, and build collaborations at the intersection of machine learning, ethical AI, and Muslim communities and contexts.

We bring together researchers, professionals, practitioners, and students to promote ML research carried out by members of the global Muslim community and conversations about AI’s impact on Muslim communities worldwide.

Grounded in advocacy, inclusivity, rigor, and integrity, we increase the visibility of trainees’ work and support students by connecting them with mentors and collaborators across academia and industry.

Our Core Values

  • Knowledge Seeking (Talab al-'Ilm)
    Emphasizing the pursuit of knowledge and lifelong learning. We encourage researchers and participants to deepen their understanding, contribute meaningfully to machine learning, and advance scientific knowledge for the benefit of society.
  • Excellence (Tafawuq)
    Striving for the highest standards in research, scholarship, community engagement, and event organization. We value rigor, quality, and continuous improvement in all our activities.
  • Innovation (Ibtikar)
    Encouraging creativity, curiosity, and the development of novel ideas. We support innovative approaches to addressing complex challenges and advancing the field of machine learning.
  • Collaboration (Mosharaka)
    Promoting meaningful partnerships across academia, industry, and communities. We believe that collective effort and knowledge sharing are essential for scientific progress and societal impact.
  • Integrity (Nazaha)
    Upholding ethical research practices, transparency, accountability, and respect for human dignity. We are committed to responsible AI development that protects privacy and avoids harm.
  • Inclusivity (Shumulia)
    Creating a welcoming environment that values diverse perspectives, backgrounds, and experiences. We foster respectful dialogue and equitable participation to ensure all voices are heard and appreciated.
